Explanation of the technology


Receiving material information from an artist's terminal, then storing this material information in a material information database while converting the material information into an artificial nucleotide sequence. Requesting the production of art material embedded with the converted artificial nucleotide sequence from a material producer's terminal. After the artwork is produced using the art material, receiving information about the artwork and registering this information in an art information database. Providing the registered artwork information to a buyer's terminal, and processing the transaction for the artwork selected by the buyer through their terminal. If nucleotide sequence information is received from the buyer's terminal, searching for this nucleotide sequence information in the material information database.
